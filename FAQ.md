# Frequently Asked Questions - lovebite.club

## General Questions

### What types of events does lovebite.club host?

We host two main types of events:

**"A Table of X" Social Mixers**
- Intimate gatherings of 6-10 singles at curated venues
- Reserved table for the group at stylish bars
- No speed dating, games, or forced interactions
- Organic conversation over drinks and nibbles
- Perfect for those who prefer relaxed, natural mingling

**Speed Dating Events**
- Meet multiple singles in one evening
- 5-7 minute one-on-one mini-dates
- Rotate smoothly to connect with new people each round
- Submit your matches at the end — mutual matches shared privately
- Friendly, guided format with professional hosts

---

### Can I come alone?

**Absolutely!** Most guests arrive solo, and our friendly hosts make the introductions easy. By the end of the night you'll feel like you've known people for ages. The small group setting and welcoming atmosphere make it comfortable for everyone, whether you're naturally outgoing or a bit shy.

---

### What is the age range for events?

We offer events for different age groups to ensure you meet people at a similar stage of life:
- **26-47 years**: Professional mixers and social gatherings
- **27-49 years**: Popular speed dating events
- **30-40 years**: Tailored speed dating for thirty-somethings
- **34-49 years**: Sophisticated social drinks
- **35-49 years**: Curated table events for established professionals

Each event listing clearly states the age range. We kindly ask attendees to stay within the specified range to ensure a comfortable and balanced experience for everyone.

---

### What if I'm slightly outside the age range?

We kindly ask attendees to stay within the specified age range to ensure a comfortable and balanced experience for everyone. If you're close to the range, feel free to contact us before registering and we can discuss on a case-by-case basis.

- No need for formal wear, but do put your best foot forward

### What should I expect at the event?

**"Table of X" Social Mixers:**

- No forced activities or awkward games

- Relaxed, natural environment

**Speed Dating Events:**

- Submit your preferences at the end

---

### What is speed dating?

Speed dating is a fun, face-to-face way to meet multiple singles in one night. You'll have a series of short, flirty one-on-one chats, then note who you'd like to see again. If it's a match, you'll be connected after the event — no swiping, no pressure, just real chemistry.

---

### How does matching work at speed dating events?

1. You'll have mini-dates with multiple singles
2. After each date, privately note if you'd like to see them again
3. Submit your preferences at the end of the night
4. If there's a mutual match (you both selected each other), we'll share contact details privately within 24 hours
5. No awkward rejections — only mutual connections are shared

---

Your comfort is our priority. Event hosts will be available throughout the evening — please don't hesitate to reach out if you need assistance. We maintain a strict code of conduct and anyone making others uncomfortable will be asked to leave.
