Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you host?

We host two main types of events: "A Table of X" Social Mixers , intimate gatherings of 6-10 singles at curated venues with reserved tables and organic conversation (no forced activities). And Speed Dating Events , where you'll meet multiple singles in one evening through 5-7 minute mini-dates, with mutual matches shared privately after the event.

Can I come alone?

Absolutely! Most guests arrive solo, and our friendly hosts make the introductions easy. By the end of the night you'll feel like you've known people for ages. The small group setting and welcoming atmosphere make it comfortable for everyone, whether you're naturally outgoing or a bit shy.

What should I wear?

Smart casual is the standard for all our events. Think "Friday night drinks" or "dinner with friends" , stylish but relaxed, comfortable and authentic to you. Our venues are carefully selected to be sophisticated yet welcoming. No need for formal wear, but do put your best foot forward.

How does speed dating matching work?

You'll have mini-dates with multiple singles throughout the evening. After each date, privately note if you'd like to see them again. Submit your preferences at the end of the night, and if there's a mutual match (you both selected each other), we'll share contact details privately within 24 hours. No awkward rejections , only mutual connections are shared.

What's included in the ticket?

Your ticket includes your place at the curated event, professional hosting throughout the evening, and access to the matched group of singles. Drinks and food are typically NOT included (purchased individually at the bar), though some events may offer happy hour specials. Each event listing specifies exactly what's included.

What if I'm outside the age range?

We offer events for different age groups (26-49) to ensure you meet people at a similar stage of life. We kindly ask attendees to stay within the specified range for a comfortable experience. If you're close to the range, feel free to contact us before registering and we can discuss on a case-by-case basis.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Tickets are generally non-refundable to secure your place and maintain balanced ratios. This policy helps us ensure committed attendees and fair gender balance. For specific circumstances, please contact us at hello@lovebite.club. We recommend checking your schedule before booking as spaces are limited and in high demand.

What if I feel uncomfortable at the event?

Your comfort is our priority. Professional hosts are available throughout the evening , please don't hesitate to reach out if you need assistance. We maintain a strict code of conduct and anyone making others uncomfortable will be asked to leave. Our events are designed to be welcoming, safe spaces for everyone.
